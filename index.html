In youth, I heard of space that bends, of time that flows. Many years on, from quarks to stars, the questions endure: what unseen matter shapes the sky, what gives mass to the proton, what bridge unites the quantum and the vast? To hear the Way at dawn— even dusk would suffice.

年少时，我听闻空间的弯曲，时间的流动。多年后，从夸克到星辰，疑问依旧：何种无形之物塑造苍穹，何以赋予质子质量，又是哪一座桥，连接量子与浩瀚？朝闻其道——至暮，亦足矣。

所属机构

Institution

高能物理研究所

Institute of High Energy Physics

中国科学院高能物理研究所是我国从事高能物理研究、先进加速器物理与技术研究及开发利用、先进射线技术与应用研究的综合性研究基地。研究所致力于建设成为国际领先的高能物理中心之一，以及具有世界先进水平的大型、综合性、多学科研究基地。

The Institute of High Energy Physics (IHEP) of the Chinese Academy of Sciences is a comprehensive research base in China engaged in high energy physics research, advanced accelerator physics and technology research and development, and advanced ray technology and application research. The institute is committed to building itself into one of the world's leading high energy physics centers and a large, comprehensive, multi-disciplinary research base with world advanced level.

理论物理室

Theoretical Physics Division

中国科学院高能物理研究所理论物理室是我国粒子物理、核物理、引力与宇宙学领域重要的理论研究和人才培养基地。目前理论室的主要研究方向包括：电弱标准模型的精确计算、超出标准模型的TeV新物理、中微子物理、暗物质与宇宙学、量子场论的非微扰方法、量子计算在高能物理中的应用等。理论物理室已成为国际公认的粒子和核物理理论研究中心，是中国科学院创新项目计划的重点之一。

The Theoretical Physics Division (TPD) of the Institute of High Energy Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, is an important theoretical research and talent training base in the fields of particle physics, nuclear physics, gravity and cosmology in China. The main research directions of TPD include: precise calculations of the electroweak standard model, TeV new physics beyond the standard model, neutrino physics, dark matter and cosmology, non-perturbative methods of quantum field theory, applications of quantum computing in high energy physics, etc. TPD has become an internationally recognized research center for particle and nuclear physics theory, and is one of the priorities of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Innovation Project Scheme.
